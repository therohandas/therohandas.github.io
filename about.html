About me

I believe that the best stories are found at the intersection of discipline and imagination. Whether I'm on center stage, on the rollball court, or diving into a new piece of technology, I am constantly looking for ways to push boundaries and connect with the world around me.

The Creative Spark

Acting is my greatest passion. To me, it's more than just performing; it's about stepping into someone else's shoes and creating moments that resonate. From theater stages to digital storytelling, I thrive on the challenge of bringing emotions to life and finding the "human" element in every script.

Competitive by Nature

My time as a member of the Odisha Rollball Skating Team and a football player has shaped my mindset. It taught me that excellence isn't a one-time actit's a habit built on discipline, perseverance, and seamless teamwork. I carry that same "game-day" energy into my leadership roles, including my three-year tenure as Secretary of the Scouts and Guides Club.

Tech & Innovation

I don't just see science and art as separate worlds; I see them as partners in shaping the future. As a member of the Chairman's Club at SAI International, I am deeply curious about how innovation can solve real-world problems. When I'm not studying or acting, you'll likely find me exploring tech-driven projects or traveling to find fresh inspiration in different cultures.
